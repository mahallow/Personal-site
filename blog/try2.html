☃ About

Hello and welcome! Below, now sparse, will be the blog entries as Heather and I go about refitting our 1977 Doug Peterson 37 sailing yacht as a liveaboard and cruising machine. Currently she is up Anacortes on the hard for the winter while we bask in the sun in SoCal and save Money. Soon we will return, go broke purchasing hardware and equipment we don't know much about, and begin setting her up in such a way that she can be more or less sustainable off grid as a home and as a travel vessel. We will update here about the projects onboard and the travels she takes us on.
